Help

1.
This application will sync with your fitbit account upon successful login.

2.
Under the "Home" page of this application, you can see your available VendFit balance, which is updated from your Fitbit account.

3.
On the "Home" page, you will see a list of available products for vending along with their cost and nutritional values.

4.
To purchase an item, you must click the applicable "Vend" button next to the desired item.

5.
After purchase, your available balance will be updated and you will be prompted to input the beverage into the food log associated with your Fitbit account.
